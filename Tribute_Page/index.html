Romanian brilliant politician, prime minister, architect of Romanian Union, martir of Communist persecutions.

Life Events

1896 - Early Years

Was born in Badacin, Salaj county. Follows the School of Law in Cluj, Budapest and Vienna. Obtains a doctorate in law in Vienna

1918 - Austro-Hungarian Empire

Lawyer in Blaj, member in Romanian National Party, deputy in Hungarian Parliament. Incorporated in WWI despite beeing 42 years old, at the end of the war is active in romanian minority rights. Was one of organizers of Great Assembly from Alba Iulia which decided the Great Union between Transylvania and Romania.

1928 - Kingdom of Romania

Onvolved in romanian politics, president on National Peasants Party, three times prime minister of Romania. His political opponent of the time was Romanian Liberal Party

1953 - Second World War and later years.

Fights for democracy and fair elections. Communists fake the elections and then arrest him. Sick, in Sighet Prison, dies at Feb 5 1953.
